PARAMORE

Paramore is a rock band formed in 2004, from FRanklin, Tennessee. The lead vocalist is Hayley Williams. They have had eleven albums: All We Know Is Falling (2004), The Summer Tic EP (2006), Riot! (2007), Live in the UK (2008), The Final Riot! (2008), Brand New Eyes (2009), 2010 Summer Tour EP (2010), Singles Clubs (2011), Paramore (2013), The Holiday Sessions (2013), and Paramore: Self-Titled Deluxe (2013).
